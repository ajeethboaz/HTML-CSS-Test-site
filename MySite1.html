Well, I am from India, a software developer by profession, learning photography as a passion. It was my dream to own a DSLR and the dream came true with the help of my friend Gopinath Sarangapani, who helped me get a Canon DSLR. After that there was no turning back, I pick up my camera hang it around my neck and start walking, I basically click anything which I find interesting. During such moments I even forget how many miles I have walked.

This site basically portrays my random clicks and crazy experiments I end up doing when am free. I believe that photography is not about expensive gear and equipment. Wonders can be done with minimal equipment Its all about one’s perspective. That being said, few moments you would feel that this shot would ve been brilliant with this lens and that gear, I’m just the same person working towards getting the perfect shot.

"I would say Photography for me has turned from a hobby to a passion"
